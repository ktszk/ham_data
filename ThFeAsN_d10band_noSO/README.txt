This is Fe 3d 10 orbital model without spin-orbital coupling.
This model is obtained using wien2k (ref. 1) and wannier90 (ref. 2) adopting experimental strucre in ref 3.

1. P. Blaha, K. Schwarz, G. K. H. Madsen, D. Kvasnicka, and J. Luitz,Wien2k: An Augmented PlaneWave + Local Orbitals Program for Calculating Crystal Properties (Vienna University of Technology, Wien, 2001):http://www.wien2k.at/
2. Wannier90: A Tool for Obtaining Maximally-Localised Wannier Functions A. A. Mostofi, J. R. Yates, Y. S. Lee, I. Souza, D. Vanderbilt and N. Marzari Comput. Phys. Commun. 178, 685 (2008):http://www.wannier.org/
3. C. Wang, Z. Wang, Y. Mei, Y. Li, L. Li, Z. Tang, Y. Liu, P. Zhang, H. Zhai, Z. Xu and G. Cao, J. Am. Chem. Soc. 138, 2170-2173 (2016)
